Version 1.4.0

 - New real time DPS meter overlay. Movable, resizable, tracks whoever you want (toggle with Alt + O).
 - New Campaign Ledger. Keeps stats for your whole run, picks a campaign nemesis, shows each character's top 3 grades, and exports to a text file (Alt + L).
 - New combat comparison table, every fighter side by side (Alt + K).
 - New session history, so you can browse your previous fights and not just the last one.
 - New dedicated block for reanimated undead thralls (Lich), kept apart from the master's own stats.
 - New dispel counter that lists the exact spells you dispelled.
 - Fully customizable keyboard shortcuts and a numeric UI scale, both in the UMM settings page (Ctrl + F10).
 - Summon, reanimated and domination stats no longer leak into their master or into the party totals.
